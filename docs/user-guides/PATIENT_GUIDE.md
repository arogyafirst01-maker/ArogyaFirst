# ArogyaFirst - Patient User Guide

## Phase 1 Scope Note

**Current Phase 1 Implementation:**

- Appointment booking (OPD, IPD, LAB) with payment integration
- Medical document upload and consent-based sharing
- Prescription viewing and management
- Booking history and status tracking
- Video consultations with doctors
- Invoice and payment receipt downloads

**Advanced Features (Phase 2/3):**

- Family account management (book for dependents)
- Appointment rescheduling (currently requires cancel + rebook)
- Health timeline and medical history visualization
- Medication reminders and adherence tracking
- Insurance integration and claims processing
- Telemedicine follow-ups and remote monitoring

Features marked as "Phase 2/3" or mentioned in FAQs as "coming soon" are planned for upcoming releases.

## Getting Started

### Registration

1. Visit ArogyaFirst website
2. Click "Register" and select "Patient"
3. Fill in required information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Phone Number
   - Location (City/State)
   - Date of Birth
   - Last 4 digits of Aadhaar
4. Click "Register"
5. Your unique Patient ID: `YourPhone@ArogyaFirst`
6. You'll be automatically logged in

### Login

1. Click "Login"
2. Enter email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. Redirected to dashboard

## Booking Appointments

### Step 1: Search for Provider

1. Click "New Booking"
2. Select booking type:
   - **OPD** - Outpatient Department
   - **IPD** - Inpatient/Bed Booking
   - **LAB** - Laboratory Tests
3. Enter location
4. Select specialization (for doctors)
5. Select date
6. Click "Search"

### Step 2: Select Provider

- Browse search results
- View provider details (name, qualifications, location)
- Click "View Slots" on preferred provider

### Step 3: Choose Slot

- Select date from calendar
- View available time slots
- Click on desired time slot
- Review slot details
- Click "Continue"

### Step 4: Enter Details

- Add reason for visit (optional)
- Add special notes
- Review booking summary
- Click "Proceed to Payment"

### Step 5: Make Payment

1. Review payment amount
2. Click "Pay Now"
3. Razorpay checkout opens
4. Choose payment method:
   - UPI
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Net Banking
5. Complete payment

### Step 6: Confirmation

- Booking confirmed!
- Note your Booking ID (e.g., BK-1234567890-A3F9)
- View booking details
- Booking added to dashboard

## Managing Bookings

### View Bookings

1. Click "Bookings" in navigation
2. View tabs:
   - **All** - All your bookings
   - **Upcoming** - Future appointments
   - **Past** - Completed appointments
   - **Cancelled** - Cancelled bookings
3. Click any booking for details

### Cancel Booking

1. Open booking details
2. Click "Cancel Booking"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Refund processed automatically (5-7 business days)
5. Status changes to CANCELLED

## Consent Management

### What is Consent?

Providers need your permission to access your medical documents. You control who can see your records.

### Approve Consent Request

1. Go to "Documents" > "Consent Requests" tab
2. View pending requests
3. Click "Review" on request
4. See provider details and reason
5. Click "Approve" to grant access
6. Set expiry duration (optional)
7. Provider can now view your documents

### Revoke Consent

1. Go to "Active Consents" tab
2. Click "Revoke" on consent
3. Confirm revocation
4. Provider immediately loses access

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Keep Profile Updated** - Current phone and location help find providers
2. **Upload Documents Regularly** - Keep all medical records in one place
3. **Manage Consents Carefully** - Only approve requests from trusted providers
4. **Book in Advance** - Popular slots fill up quickly
5. **Save Booking IDs** - Keep for reference when visiting providers
6. **Cancel Early** - Early cancellation ensures full refund

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login

- Verify email and password
- Check Caps Lock
- Try "Forgot Password"
- Clear browser cache

### Payment Failed

- Check internet connection
- Verify payment details
- Try different payment method
- Booking remains in PENDING status

### Document Upload Failed

- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Verify file type (PDF, JPG, PNG only)
- Check internet connection

### Refund Not Received

- Refunds take 5-7 business days
- Check refund status in booking details
- Contact bank after 7 days
- Contact support with Booking ID

## FAQs

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry best practices.

**Q: Can I book for family members?**  
A: Currently, each person needs their own account. Family accounts coming in Phase 2.

**Q: How do I get a refund?**  
A: Cancel your booking before the appointment. Refund is automatic (5-7 business days).

**Q: Can doctors see all my documents?**  
A: No, only after you approve their consent request.

**Q: What if I miss my appointment?**  
A: Booking marked as NO_SHOW. No refund for missed appointments.

**Q: Can I reschedule?**  
A: Currently, cancel and book again. Rescheduling coming soon.
